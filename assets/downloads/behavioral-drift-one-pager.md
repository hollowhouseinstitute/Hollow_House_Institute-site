## 1. Concept Overview

**Behavioral Drift** refers to the phenomenon where either **AI systems or human users interacting with AI** begin to deviate from expected behaviors over time. Drift can emerge due to:

- Repeated exposure to AI guidance affecting decision-making.
- Systemic feedback loops that subtly change operational patterns.
- Environmental, contextual, or organizational changes that interact with AI outputs.

**Goal:** Identify, measure, and correct drift to ensure AI-human alignment, maintain trust, and prevent operational risk.

---

## 2. Relevance

Behavioral Drift is critical because:

- Undetected drift can lead to unintended consequences.
- It can erode governance structures and compliance adherence.
- Drift represents an early warning for emerging risks in longitudinal AI interactions.

## 4. Key Metrics / KPIs

1. **Drift Percentage:** Proportion of actions deviating from baseline expectations.
2. **Frequency of Deviations:** How often drift events occur per time unit.
3. **Corrective Actions Taken:** Number and effectiveness of interventions.
4. **Decision Accuracy Impact:** Effect of drift on key operational outcomes.
5. **Policy Compliance Rate:** Percentage of actions aligned with governance rules.

---

## 5. Recommended Artifacts

- **Drift Monitoring Dashboard:** Visualize deviations in real-time.
- **Audit Log Schema:** Timestamped, immutable logs for all AI-human interactions.
- **Corrective Action Reports:** Documentation of detected drift and response steps.
- **Case Studies:** Examples of drift detection, mitigation, and outcomes.

---

## 6. Implementation Guidance

1. **Establish Baseline:** Define expected behavior for both AI outputs and human actions interacting with AI.  
2. **Continuous Monitoring:** Deploy automated and human-reviewed systems to detect deviations.  
3. **Threshold Definition:** Set clear criteria for what constitutes significant drift.  
4. **Corrective Mechanisms:** Implement process adjustments, retraining, or governance interventions.  
5. **Review Cycle:** Quarterly assessment and reporting to ensure alignment with governance policies.
